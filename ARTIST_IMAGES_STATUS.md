## Image Requirements

- **Size**: 400x400 or 500x500 pixels (square)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Location**: Save to `assets/images/artists/`

## Quick Download Instructions

1. Click on the search link for each artist
2. Find a good quality, recent photo
3. Download and rename to the required filename
4. Save to `assets/images/artists/` folder

- **Instagram**: Most artists have official Instagram accounts

- **Official Websites**: Artist management sites
- **Music Platforms**: Spotify, Apple Music artist pages

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Use royalty-free or press/promo images when possible
- Avoid copyrighted images without permission
- For production apps, consider licensing official photos
- Memorial listings (Lucky Dube, AKA, Riky Rick) - use respectful, iconic photos
