Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your Gearsh account and data.

Important: This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. This includes your profile, booking history, messages, and any other information associated with your account.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Gearsh account using either of the following methods:

Method 1: Delete from the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly from the Gearsh app:

  1. Open the Gearsh app on your device
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down to Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  6. Tap Permanently Delete to confirm

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to support@gearsh.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your Gearsh account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days
Send Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, photo, bio, etc.)
  • All booking history
  • Messages and conversations
  • Reviews you've written
  • Saved artists and preferences
  • Payment method information
  • All associated data and activity
Note: Some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as financial transaction records for tax purposes). This retained data will be anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

Before You Delete

Consider the following before deleting your account:

  • Active Bookings: Complete or cancel any pending bookings first
  • Pending Payments: Ensure all payments have been processed
  • Download Your Data: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Alternative: You can temporarily deactivate instead of permanently deleting
